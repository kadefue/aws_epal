Our Focus Areas

The EPA Lab is dedicated to advancing agriculture through technology. Our key activities revolve around Research, Training, Consultancy, and Outreach in electronics and precision agriculture.

IoT for Agriculture

Developing smart solutions for real-time monitoring and management of agricultural resources using Internet of Things technology.

AI & Data Analytics

Leveraging Artificial Intelligence and data analytics to provide insights for crop management, disease detection, and yield optimization.

Agricultural Robotics & Drones

Exploring the use of robotics and drone technology for precision field operations, mapping, and data collection.
