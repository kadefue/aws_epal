Our Research and Innovations

The EPA Lab focuses on developing and applying ICT-based solutions to address challenges in the agricultural sector. Our research spans several key areas, leading to innovative products and services.

Internet of Things (IoT) for Smart Agriculture

We design and deploy IoT-based systems for real-time monitoring of soil nutrients, environmental conditions, and water resources. This includes innovations like Solar Powered Smart Water Pumping Systems for Irrigation and IoT Based Real-Time Soil Nutrient and Environmental Monitoring Systems, enabling data-driven farming decisions.

Keywords: IoT, smart farming, sensor networks, water management, soil monitoring

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Analytics

Our work in AI involves developing intelligent systems for pest and disease detection, crop health monitoring, and yield prediction. The AI-Powered Plant Pest and Disease Detection System is one such innovation, aiming to provide early warnings to farmers.

Keywords: Artificial Intelligence, machine learning, data analytics, pest detection, disease diagnosis

Agricultural Robotics and Drone Technology (SUADRONELAB)

Through initiatives like SUADRONELAB, we explore the application of drones and robotics for precision agriculture tasks such as aerial imaging, crop spraying, and field mapping. This technology helps in optimizing input use and improving farm management efficiency.

Keywords: Drones, UAV, agricultural robotics, precision agriculture, aerial mapping

Agricultural Platforms and Advisory Services

We develop platforms like the SUA AgMe Platform to provide farmers with access to crucial information, including weather forecasts, market prices, and agricultural advisory services, fostering informed decision-making.

Keywords: Mobile applications, advisory services, market information, weather data
