About The EPA Laboratory

Establishment

The Electronics and Precision Agriculture Laboratory (EPA Lab) at Sokoine University of Agriculture (SUA) stemmed from the EPASSA project (Electronics and Precision Agriculture for Sokoine University of Agriculture). This initiative was generously funded by the Norwegian government with the aim to enhance teaching, research, and outreach capabilities in the crucial fields of electronics and precision agriculture.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in electronics and precision agriculture for sustainable agricultural development in Tanzania and beyond.

Our Mission

To advance and apply knowledge and skills in electronics and precision agriculture through innovative research, training, consultancy, and outreach services to address societal challenges and promote agricultural transformation.

Core Values

  • Innovation
  • Excellence
  • Collaboration
  • Integrity
  • Inclusivity
  • Sustainability

Objectives

  • Conduct cutting-edge research in electronics and precision agriculture.
  • Develop and offer demand-driven training programs for various stakeholders.
  • Provide expert consultancy and outreach services to the agricultural community.
  • Foster robust national and international collaborations with academic institutions, research organizations, and industry partners.
  • Develop and promote innovative technologies and solutions for practical application in agriculture.
